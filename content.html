This could happen while in transit during which the conditions are out of our control. Over-ripening of the mangoes can also occur sometimes due to unfavorable conditions. Our order fulfillment is completely open and transparent to ensure that nobody has an undesirable experience. Despite that since its a perishable produce and due to factors beyond our control you might have received a spoilt fruit. We are aware of such situations arising and stand by them completely. Once the order is placed, the mango boxes will be dispatched at the earliest possible date subject to availability. Fruit weight is always measured at harvest and grading is done at that time. By the time you receive the mangoes, 15 to 20% weight loss can occur due to moisture loss while ripening.
